David's Vault

What is David's Vault?

David's Vault is a weekly radio show that airs Mondays from 10:30 pm to midnight cst on WIIT. Hosted by David Delgado, the show is a curation of music usually within the hip-hop/rap genre.

What am I signing up for?

When you sign up for my newsletter, you will be getting weekly emails of the following:

  • David's Vault weekly tracklist
  • Personalized tracklist based on your favorite genre
  • News announcements from your favorite artists
  • Special tokens to request songs for next week's show
